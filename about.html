About Stackly

We are on a mission to democratize finance for all, providing institutional-grade tools to retail traders globally.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of former Wall Street quantitative analysts, Stackly was born out of frustration with the archaic and expensive trading platforms available to the public.

We spent years developing a proprietary matching engine and sleek user interface that brings institutional speed and reliability to your fingertips. Today, we process millions of trades daily and hold licenses in multiple top-tier jurisdictions.
