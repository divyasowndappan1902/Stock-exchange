Platform Update: Mastering the New Options Chains Interface

Sarah Jenkins

Head of Product, Stackly

We are thrilled to roll out the latest feature update for Stackly Pro: a completely re-engineered options trading dashboard. Based on valuable feedback from our professional and retail trading community, we have replaced our traditional layout with a lightning-fast, highly customizable Options Chain UI designed to speed up setup evaluation and execution.

This tutorial walks you through the core enhancements and demonstrates how to leverage the new interface for multi-leg strategies.

"Speed and visualization are critical when trading options. Our new options engine displays real-time Greek computations and payoff projections with zero lag, allowing traders to evaluate risk before clicking execute." — Sarah Jenkins, Head of Product

1. The Unified Options Chain

In our previous layout, calls and puts were split into separate tabs, forcing traders to toggle back and forth to inspect straddles and spreads. The new layout brings a unified grid format: Calls are placed on the left, Strike Prices are highlighted in the center, and Puts are on the right.

This layout mimics institutional terminals. Every row lists the Last Trade Price, Net Change, Bid/Ask spreads, Volatility (IV), and the delta values for easy structural alignment. You can customize which column widgets are active in your settings panel.

2. Multi-Leg Builder Integration

Executing multi-leg option strategies (such as Iron Condors, Verticals, and Calendars) previously required manually opening separate order tickets. Now, holding the Ctrl key (or long-pressing on mobile) while clicking an option Bid or Ask price automatically adds that contract as a leg to the Strategy Builder at the bottom of the screen.

The builder tracks your net debit or credit in real-time, displays the aggregate margin requirements, and supports one-click, multi-leg market or limit order routing.

3. Real-Time Payoff Chart Overlay

One of the most requested features is the interactive payoff graph. When configuring spreads in the builder, the chart updates dynamically to display profit and loss bounds based on expiration dates and price zones.

This visual indicator overlay helps you locate high-probability setups by drawing green shades for profitable zones and red shades for risk boundaries, ensuring clear confirmation before submitting the trade ticket.

Summary of Key Interface Hotkeys

  • Left-Click (or Tap): Select option line to pull up detail cards and Greek variables.
  • Ctrl + Click: Queue the contract leg into the Multi-Leg Strategy Builder.
  • Right-Click (Hold): Toggle trade parameters (buy/sell, calls/puts, size).
